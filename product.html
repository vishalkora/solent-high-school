Our Sesame Seeds

Sesame Seeds is a flowering plant, which is cultivated for its edible seeds, which are the source of numerous essential nutrients such as Vitamin B1, calcium, magnesium, copper, dietary fiber, zinc etc. Sesame is a tall annual plant, which belongs to the Pedaliaceae family. Varieties of sesame are adapted according to soil nature.
